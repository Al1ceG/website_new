Welcome to Hoppers!

Edinburgh Hoppers is the official society for women, non-binary and transgender people in the School of Informatics of the University of Edinburgh

As the official society for Gender Minorities in Informatics at the University of Edinburgh our goal is to cultivate an open community with the aim to provide a supportive and inclusive society where people are comfortable and build both technical and networking skills to thrive in the tech environment.

Our focus is to empower all women, non-binaries and transgender in technology.
